PHN TCH D LIU

B d liu Black Friday Sales cha thng tin chi tit v cc giao dch bn hng trong ngy Black Friday. Vic phn tch d liu ny s gip chng ta hiu r hn v hnh vi mua sm ca khch hng, t c th d on s tin mua sm v xy dng cc chin lc tip th hiu qu.

Phn tch d liu chun b cho vic d on s tin mua sm

Di y l mt s phn tch d liu c bn gip chun b cho vic d on s tin mua sm ca khch hng:

a. Phn tch phn phi s tin mua sm (Purchase Distribution):

  • Kim tra phn phi ca s tin mua sm hiu r hn v hnh vi chi tiu ca khch hng. Biu histogram gip chng ta thy r phn phi ca d liu v xc nh cc gi tr ngoi lai (outliers).

b. Phn tch s tin mua sm theo nhm tui (Purchase by Age Group):

  • S dng biu box plot so snh phn phi s tin mua sm gia cc nhm tui. iu ny gip chng ta xc nh nhm tui no c xu hng chi tiu nhiu hn.

c. Phn tch mi quan h gia s tin mua sm v tui (Purchase vs. Age):

  • S dng biu scatter plot kim tra mi quan h gia tui v s tin mua sm. iu ny gip chng ta hiu r hn v cch tui nh hng n hnh vi mua sm.

d. Phn tch s lng giao dch theo gii tnh (Transactions by Gender):

  • S dng biu thanh m s lng giao dch theo gii tnh. iu ny gip chng ta xc nh gii tnh no c xu hng mua sm nhiu hn.

e. Phn tch t l cc loi thnh ph (City Category):

  • S dng biu trn hin th t l cc loi thnh ph. iu ny gip chng ta hiu r hn v phn b a l ca cc giao dch.

Li ch ca vic phn tch d liu vi Numpy, Pandas v Matplotlib

Vic s dng Numpy, Pandas v Matplotlib trong phn tch d liu mang li nhiu li ch cho mt Data Analyst, bao gm:

  • X l d liu hiu qu: Numpy v Pandas cung cp cc cng c mnh m thao tc v x l d liu mt cch nhanh chng v hiu qu.
  • Phn tch d liu chi tit: Cc hm thng k v nhm d liu trong Pandas gip phn tch d liu chi tit, t hiu r hn v cc c trng v mi quan h trong d liu.
  • Trc quan ha d liu: Matplotlib cung cp cc cng c trc quan ha d liu phong ph, gip truyn t thng tin mt cch r rng v trc quan.

Tm quan trng ca vic phn tch d liu

Phn tch d liu ng vai tr quan trng trong vic hiu r hn v hnh vi mua sm ca khch hng, t gip:

  • D on v ti u ha doanh s bn hng: Hiu r hn v cc yu t nh hng n s tin mua sm gip d on doanh s bn hng chnh xc hn v ti u ha chin lc bn hng.
  • Xy dng chin lc tip th hiu qu: Phn tch hnh vi mua sm gip xy dng cc chin lc tip th nhm ng i tng khch hng v tng cng hiu qu tip th.
  • Qun l v ci thin dch v khch hng: Hiu r hn v nhu cu v hnh vi ca khch hng gip ci thin dch v khch hng v tng cng s hi lng ca khch hng.

Vi nhng phn tch ny, chng ta c th a ra cc quyt nh da trn d liu ci thin hiu qu kinh doanh v tng cng tri nghim khch hng.
